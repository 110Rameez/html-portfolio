The Best Movies According to Rameez

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time.

The Pursuit of Happiness

This movie depicts the hardship a common man face in major aspects of his life to bring peace in his life.

Life of Pi

The movie shows the strength of an ordinary young man who was caught into an accident and had to dealt
with life threatening challenges in the midst of the ocean completely on his own on a life boat.

The Wolves of Wall Street

This masterpiece depicts the life of a business hungry young man who understand the psycology of the masses
and use that to make fortune out of nothing by just manipulating the greed factor of human emotions.
